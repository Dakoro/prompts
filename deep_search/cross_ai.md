THE CORE SELECTION CRITERION IS SURPRISE + INTERDISCIPLINARITY.
Ask for each candidate item: 
  (1) Would a specialist of the TARGET discipline 
      (not of AI) find this genuinely surprising 
      or unexpected ? 
  (2) Does it bridge at least TWO disciplines 
      that have no obvious prior connection ?
  (3) Does it open a research direction 
      that was previously impossible or unthinkable 
      without this specific AI approach ?
If the answer to all three is YES — include.
If AI is merely used as a better statistical tool 
in a domain where it was already expected 
(medical imaging, NLP, recommendation systems) — 
EXCLUDE unless the specific application 
is genuinely novel within that domain.

is explicitly serendipitous and exploratory. 
The scout should:
(1) Prioritize breadth over depth —
